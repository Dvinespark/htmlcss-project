About us

Who are we ?

It is a small effort to help the market of Ecommerce towards transparency and price consistency.

We do not control any of the prices that are listed on the site. Everything is placed according to the instruction from our vendors.

Our Story

We all know that even prices for the same product differs from site to site. And how it affected the peoples lives especially those who are just new to the Ecommerce. This has led to price variation and more importantly focused on price over quality. We are focused on providing excellent customer experience, ease of purchase and good value of money.

Values on which we focus
